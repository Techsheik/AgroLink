================================================================================
AGROLINK NIGERIA - COMPLETE WEBSITE FEATURES & GUIDE
================================================================================

1. CORE PLATFORM ARCHITECTURE
-----------------------------
*   **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)**: Distinct dashboards and permissions for Farmers, Buyers, and Admins.
*   **7-Day Free Trial**: New users get full access for 7 days to explore the platform.
*   **Premium Subscription**: Monthly fee of 2,000 Naira for continued full access after the trial.
*   **Security**: JWT-based authentication and secure password hashing.
*   **Dark Mode**: Full support for light and dark themes across all pages.

2. FARMER FEATURES
------------------
*   **Crop Management**: Add, edit, and delete crop listings (Grains, Tubers, Fruits, etc.).
*   **Inventory Control**: Mark crops as sold out or update available quantities.
*   **Smart Buyer Matching**: AI-powered suggestions of potential buyers based on crop type and region.
*   **Payment Verification**: View uploaded payment receipts from buyers and verify them before shipping.
*   **Delivery Management**: Update order status (Processing -> Shipped -> In Transit -> Delivered).
*   **Direct Chat**: Real-time communication with interested buyers.
*   **Analytics**: Track total revenue, active listings, and new purchase requests.

3. BUYER FEATURES
------------------
*   **Marketplace Browsing**: Search and filter crops from verified farmers across Nigeria.
*   **Purchase Requests**: Send formal requests to farmers with custom quantities and messages.
*   **Payment Workflow**: Upload proof of bank transfer (receipts) directly to the order.
*   **Delivery Tracking**: Monitor the 5-stage delivery process in real-time with a visual stepper.
*   **Confirm Receipt**: Securely signify when a delivery has been received to finalize the deal.
*   **Saved Listings**: Bookmark interesting crops to compare or purchase later.
*   **Chat System**: Negotiate directly with farmers via the built-in messaging center.

4. ADMIN COMMAND CENTER
-----------------------
*   **Platform Dashboard**: Dynamic statistics on users, crops, revenue, and regional activity.
*   **User Verification**: Review National Identity Number (NIN) uploads to verify new accounts.
*   **Subscription Control**: Manually extend any user's trial or subscription (add +7 or +30 days).
*   **Global Moderation**: Oversight of all crop listings and user accounts.
*   **Regional Analytics**: View top-performing states and LGAs in terms of trade volume.

5. SMART INTELLIGENCE
---------------------
*   **Market Price Trends**: Automated calculation of average prices per crop category.
*   **Daily Price Change Index**: Compares today's prices with yesterday's to show trends (Up/Down/Stable).
*   **Regional Proximity Matching**: Connects farmers and buyers within the same geographical area.

6. TECHNICAL STACK
------------------
*   **Backend**: Python (FastAPI) + SQLAlchemy ORM.
*   **Frontend**: React (Vite) + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS.
*   **Database**: PostgreSQL (Supabase) with optimized connection pooling.
*   **Cloud Storage**: Supabase Storage for NIN cards and payment receipts.
