AgroLink Nigeria - Platform Features & Documentation
===================================================

AgroLink is a comprehensive, AI-powered agricultural marketplace designed specifically for the Nigerian ecosystem. It connects farmers directly with buyers, provides market intelligence, and streamlines the entire trade lifecycle from planting to delivery.

1. User Roles & Access Control (RBAC)
-------------------------------------
* Farmer: Can list crops, manage inventory, receive purchase requests, chat with buyers, and track deliveries.
* Buyer: Can browse the marketplace, save crops, send purchase requests, upload payment proof, and track their orders.
* Admin: Oversees the platform, verifies new users (NIN verification), manages listings, and monitors system stats.

2. Localized Identity Verification (NIN)
-----------------------------------------
* Registration requires uploading a National Identity Number (NIN) card image.
* Farmers and Buyers are placed in a "Pending Verification" state upon registration.
* Admins must manually review the NIN and profile details (State of Origin, LGA) before granting full access to the dashboard.

3. AI-Powered Smart Matching & Recommendations
----------------------------------------------
* Buyer Recommendations: Suggests crops to buyers based on their location and historical popular trends.
* Farmer Recommendations: Suggests potential buyers to farmers based on proximity and buyer interest in similar crops.
* AI Crop Advisor: (For Farmers) Provides insights on what crops to plant based on farm size, soil type, and location.

4. Dynamic Digital Marketplace
------------------------------
* Real-time Listings: Farmers can list crops with images, prices, and quantities.
* Search & Filter: Buyers can filter crops by category, location, and price.
* "Save for Later": Buyers can save interesting listings to their personal watchlist.

5. Secure Trade & Payment Lifecycle
------------------------------------
* Purchase Requests: Buyers send formal requests to farmers to initiate a trade.
* Real-time Chat: Integrated messaging system allows farmers and buyers to negotiate concurrently.
* Bank Transfer Integration: Once a request is accepted, buyers upload a screenshot of their bank transfer receipt.
* Payment Verification: Farmers verify the receipt before initiating the delivery process.

6. Transparent Delivery Tracking System
---------------------------------------
* Delivery Stepper: A visual 5-step progress bar (Pending -> Shipped -> In Transit -> Delivered -> Confirmed).
* Farmer Controls: Farmers push status updates as the goods move through the logistics chain.
* Buyer Confirmation: Buyers signify successful receipt, which finalizes the transaction status to "Completed".

7. Market Intelligence & Price Index
------------------------------------
* Live Price Tracking: Real-time dashboard showing average prices for major crops (Maize, Rice, Cocoa, etc.).
* Trend Analysis: 24h price changes and visual trend indicators (Up/Down) to help users time their sales/purchases.

8. Monetization & Subscription Model
-------------------------------------
* 7-Day Free Trial: New users get full access for 7 days to explore the platform.
* Premium Subscription: A fee of ₦2,000/month is required after the trial to continue listing and requesting.
* Admin Management: Admins can manually extend trials or subscriptions for individual users.

9. Nigeria-Centric Design & Infrastructure
-------------------------------------------
* Regional Logic: Built-in support for all 36 States and LGAs.
* Dark Mode Support: Users can toggle between light and dark modes for better readability.
* Direct IP Routing: Backend optimized for stability in various network conditions across Nigeria.

How It Works (Summary)
----------------------
1. Sign Up: Register and upload your NIN.
2. Verify: Wait for Admin approval (Safety first).
3. Connect: Use AI recommendations or browse the marketplace.
4. Trade: Send/Accept requests and chat to finalize details.
5. Pay: Upload payment proof securely.
6. Deliver: Track the journey of your goods in real-time.
7. Confirm: Finalize the deal once goods arrive.
